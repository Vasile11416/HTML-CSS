Quick Facts on Kaaterskill Falls

1. The two cascades total 260 feet in height, the longest at 180 feet.

2. Tallest cascading waterfall in New York State.

3. It's 231 feet tall which means it's 90 feet taller than Niagara Falls (167 feet)

4. One of the tallest waterfalls in the Eastern United States.

My Experience at Kaaterskill Falls

My experience at Kaaterskill Falls was spectacular. It was early november when we set out on the 2.5 hour drive. We reached the falls just before the afternoon buzz and quickly decended down the trail. Steps, so many steps. We veered off the main trail down a slippery path and reached the first falls. As I walked closer to the falls, I began to feel the mist on my cheeks, chilly, but I just kept walking. As I got closer, I paused, I got down on one knee and snapped this pic. I realized after but I was so excited to be there that I forgot my smile, close enough though :)
